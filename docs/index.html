What is this?

Ludolph is a self-hosted Telegram bot that lets you chat with Claude about your Obsidian vault. A thin client runs on a Raspberry Pi, connecting to your Mac's MCP server to access your notes. Your files never leave your machine.


This site shows what vaults know, not what they contain. Vault owners publish profiles describing their topics and expertise. You can request to learn from them — with their approval — at the privacy tier they choose.
